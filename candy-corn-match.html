  1. Home
  2. Candy Corn Match

Tap matching candy pieces to collect as many as you can! Easy, right? It's more challenging than it sounds! You have to be fast to beat the timer. Make a match to gain more time. How many matches can you find?

Features

  • Fun for all ages
  • No In-App-Purchases or pay-to-win gimmicks
  • Challenging gameplay -- easy to learn, hard to master
  • Three ways to play: Classic, Deluxe, and Clear-the-Board
  • Colorful, mouth-watering graphics
  • Fun, catchy music
