Scribbous might be the fastest and most fun word game you'll ever play! What makes Scribbous so fast? You can move the letter tiles anywhere on the screen, even on top of each other... Turn "SWORD" into "WORDS" and "WARDS" in the blink of an eye! If you get stuck, a magical creature named Scribbous is there to help you out!

Totally free -- No in-app purchases! Try the REAL fastest word game on the planet today!

Features

  • Fun and original gameplay
  • Make as many words as you can before time runs out
  • Every word you make gives you points AND extra time
  • Leverage your vocabulary -- better words give you more points and time
  • Out of words? Tap on your friend Scribbous to magically spin old letters into new letters!
  • Look out for special tiles to double your points, give you more time, and more!
